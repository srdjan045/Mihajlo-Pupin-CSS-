Literary work

Besides his patents he published several dozen scientific disputes, articles, reviews and a 396-page autobiography under the name
Michael Pupin, From Immigrant to Inventor (Scribner's, 1923). He won the annual Pulitzer Prize for Biography or Autobiography.
It was published in Serbian in 1929 under the title From pastures to scientist (Od pašnjaka do naučenjaka).
